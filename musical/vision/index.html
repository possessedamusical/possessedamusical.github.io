Creative Vision

"I love the way the staging is set against the Tristan and Isolde windows, perfectly mirroring the 3 characters and encapsulating the whole Love and Death theme which runs under all that late romantic period. I can see a stage glowing with these multi layered images and one scene bleeding into another as ghosts and mist rise – there are shades of Turn of the Screw, Wuthering Heights and Bram Stoker. I think it could be magical."

David Rogers – BAFTA award winning Designer of theatre & opera.
